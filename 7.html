Dairy Free Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

3 cups Plain Flour
1 Tbsp Baking Powder
6 Tbsp Cocoa Powder
2 cups Chelsea White Sugar
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tsp Vanilla Essence
2 Tbsp Vinegar
½ cup of Cooking Oil
1 cup Water

Instructions

Pre heat oven to 170°C and grease a cake tin.
Sift the dry ingredients into a large bowl twice
Add in liquids and mix well.
Pour in the batter into previously prepared tin.
Bake for 55 minutes or until the skewer comes out clean.
Enjoy!
