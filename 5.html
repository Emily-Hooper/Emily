Gluten Free Brownies

Ingredients

110g butter
1 cup Chelsea Soft Brown Sugar
½ cup Chelsea Caster Sugar
1½ tsp vanilla essence
3 eggs
¾ cup gluten free flour
½ cup gluten free cocoa powder
½ tsp gluten free baking powder

Instructions

Preheat oven to 160°C, grease and line a square tin with baking paper.
Melt butter, mix in sugar and vanilla. Beat in eggs one at a time. Add combined flour, cocoa and baking powder and mix thoroughly.
Pour into prepared tin.
Bake for 25 - 35 minutes or until just cooked.
Cool for 5 minutes in the tin before turning out onto a wire rack to finish cooling.
Enjoy!
