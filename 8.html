Gluten Free Chcolate Cake

Ingredients

2 cups Edmonds Gluten Free Flour
½ cup cocoa powder
1½ tsp Edmonds Baking Powder
1½ tsp Edmonds Baking Soda
1½ cups Chelsea Caster Sugar
½ tsp salt
2 eggs
1 cup Meadow Fresh Original Milk
½ cup Olivani Pure Olive Oil
1 cup boiling water

Buttercream

125gm butter, softened
1½ cup Chelsea Icing Sugar
1 tbsp Meadow Fresh Original Milk

Instructions

Pre heat oven to 180°C (160°C fan-forced) and grease and line two 20cm round springform cake tins with baking paper.
Sift all the dry ingredients into a large mixing bowl, before adding the eggs, Meadow Fresh Original Milk and Olivani Pure Olive Oil.
Whisk to a smooth batter before adding the boiling water.
Pour batter into prepared cake tin and bake for 60 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
Remove from oven and leave in tin for 10 minutes before releasing and leaving to cool on a wire rack.
cooled, cut off domed centre of cake and slice the cake in half to give you 2 flat discs.
Prepare the buttercream icing by beating softened Tararua Butter on high speed until pale before gradually adding the remaining ingredients.
Enjoy!
