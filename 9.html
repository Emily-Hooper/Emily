Vegan Chcolate Cake

Ingredients

1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
¼ cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 cup water
⅓ cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Lightly grease a cake tin.
Sift flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
Add water, oil, vanilla, and vinegar; mix together until smooth.
Pour mixture into the prepared tin.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes.
Enjoy!
