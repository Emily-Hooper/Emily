Dairy Free Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

158 g dairy free butter, softened.
½ cup granulated sugar
½ cup light brown sugar
1 egg
1 tbsp vanilla extract
¾ tsp salt
¾ tsp baking soda
2 cups all-purpose flour
½-¾ cup dairy free chocolate chips or chopped chocolate

Instructions


Preheat oven to 180°C and line 2 baking sheets with baking paper.
In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
In another bowl, combine butter and sugars, mixing until light and fluffy (1-2 minutes).
Add in the egg and vanilla extract, and mix until just combined (do not over-mix!).
Add in the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Toss in the chocolate and mix until just combined.
Use a medium cookie scoop or spoon to make 1.5-2 tablespoon sized balls and place them on the baking sheet.
Bake the cookies (one sheet at a time for even baking) for 8-11 minutes. Remove them from the oven when the edges look set.
They will look under-baked in the middle! Remove from the oven and leave them on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely.
Enjoy!
